# What are you looking for in your next role?

## English

I'm looking for three things.

**First, growth.** I've been at Danal for over four years, and while I've grown technically, I've hit a ceiling in terms of the scope of problems I can tackle. I want to work on something that pushes me beyond my comfort zone.

**Second, impact.** At a startup like Zonagent, every engineer's contribution matters. I want to be somewhere my work directly shapes the product and the company's trajectory.

**Third, global exposure.** I've spent my entire career in Korea. Working with a US-based team, solving problems for the American market—that's a new challenge I'm ready for.

Zonagent checks all three boxes.
