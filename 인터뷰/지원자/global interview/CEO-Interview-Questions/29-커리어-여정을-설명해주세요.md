## English Question

**Walk me through your career journey.**

## English Answer

My career has been a progression from writing code to leading teams and shaping systems.

**Early Career (2019-2021): Backend Developer**
I started at Danal as a backend developer working on payment processing systems. I learned the fundamentals: how to write reliable code, optimize database queries, and build systems that can't afford to fail. Every transaction I processed was someone's real money.

**Growth Phase (2021-2023): Senior Engineer**
I took ownership of critical systems. Led the migration from legacy infrastructure to containerized services. Started asking "why" more than "how"—questioning architectural decisions, proposing improvements. This is when I developed my DevOps instincts.

**Leadership Phase (2023-Present): DevOps Squad Tech Leader**
I moved from individual contributor to leading a team. My focus shifted from writing code to enabling others: setting technical direction, removing blockers, building culture. I led our Kubernetes adoption and CI/CD transformation.

**The Common Thread:**
Throughout my career, I've been drawn to problems that require both technical depth and systems thinking. I don't just want to write features—I want to understand how everything fits together.

**What's Next:**
I'm ready to take these skills global, work on a new domain, and build from the ground up at a startup.

**성장 단계 (2021-2023): 시니어 엔지니어**
중요 시스템의 오너십을 가졌습니다. 레거시 인프라에서 컨테이너화된 서비스로의 마이그레이션을 이끌었습니다. "어떻게"보다 "왜"를 더 묻기 시작했습니다—아키텍처 결정에 의문을 제기하고 개선을 제안했습니다. 이때 DevOps 본능을 개발했습니다.
