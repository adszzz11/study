# 영어 커뮨니케이션에 얼마나 자신있나요

## English Question

**How confident are you in your English communication skills?**

## 질문 번역

영어 커뮤니케이션 능력에 얼마나 자신 있으신가요?

## 한글 답변

업무보다도 영어 커뮤니케이션이 제겐 가장 큰 허들일 것 같습니다.

듣고 이해할 수 있지만, fluent하게 말할 수 있는지는 잘 모르겠습니다. Mark님도 이 부분을 염려했고, 그를 위해 현재 꾸준히 영어 스터디를 진행 중입니다.

아마 업무에 적응하면 더 폭발적으로 성장하고, 빠르게 적응하여 지장이 없도록 할 수 있을 것 같습니다.

---

## English Answer

More than the work itself, English communication will probably be my biggest hurdle.

I can listen and understand, but I'm not sure if I can speak fluently. Mark was also concerned about this, and for that reason I'm currently doing consistent English study.

I believe once I adapt to the work, I'll grow explosively and adapt quickly so it won't be an obstacle.

---

## 이전 예시 답변

I'll be honest with you—my English is functional, not perfect.

I can communicate technical concepts clearly. I've written documentation, participated in international conferences, and collaborated with overseas vendors. In written communication especially, I'm confident.

For verbal communication, I may need a moment to find the right words sometimes. But I've learned that clarity matters more than fluency. I'd rather speak slowly and be understood than speak quickly and create confusion.

What I can promise:

**First, I'll improve rapidly.** Immersion is the fastest way to learn. Working daily with a US team, my English will improve significantly within months.

**Second, I'll over-communicate to compensate.** If there's any ambiguity, I'll clarify. I'll confirm understanding. I'll write things down.

**Third, I won't let language be a bottleneck.** If I can't express something verbally, I'll sketch it, write it, or find another way. The goal is effective communication, and there are many paths to that goal.

I'd rather be honest about this than pretend it's not a factor. But I'm confident it won't prevent me from being effective.
