# 왜 미국 회사에서 일하고 싶으신가요

## English Question

**Why do you want to work for a US company?**

## 질문 번역

왜 미국 회사에서 일하고 싶으신가요?

---

## English Answer

Three reasons:

**1. Growth Beyond My Current Ceiling**
I've spent my entire career in Korea. I've grown a lot, but I've reached a plateau. Working with a US team, solving problems for the American market, communicating in English daily—these are new challenges that will push me to grow in ways that aren't possible staying in Korea.

**2. Different Problem Scale and Approach**
US tech companies, especially startups, often operate differently:
- More direct communication culture
- Faster iteration cycles
- Different approaches to product development
- Exposure to different markets and user behaviors

I want to learn from these differences.

**3. The Specific Opportunity**
Honestly, it's not just "any US company." It's Zonagent specifically. The problem you're solving is interesting. The technical challenges are real. The stage is right for someone who wants to make an impact.

If this were just about working for a US company, there are easier paths. This is about the specific opportunity to build something meaningful with a team that's tackling a genuinely hard problem.

**What I Offer:**
- Perspective from a different market (Asia)
- Discipline from working in highly-regulated fintech
- Hunger to prove myself in a new environment

I know there's risk in hiring someone from Korea. I'm betting on myself that I can deliver.
