# 왜 현재 회사를 떠나려 하나요

## English Question

**Why are you looking to leave your current company?**

## 질문 번역

왜 현재 회사를 떠나려고 하시나요?

---

## 한글 답변

떠나려는 것이 아니라, 더 큰 성장을 향해 나아가려는 것입니다.

현재 한국 금융사에서 5년간 일하면서 결제 시스템, 인프라 현대화, 팀 문화 구축 등 많은 것을 배웠습니다. 하지만 최근 몇 가지 한계를 느끼게 되었습니다:

**1. 혁신의 속도**
금융권 특성상 보안 규정과 레거시 시스템으로 인해 새로운 기술 도입이나 프로세스 개선에 상당한 제약이 있습니다.

**2. 성장의 천장**
테크 리드로서 팀 능률 개선, 신기술 세미나 등을 주도했지만, 조직 구조상 더 큰 영향력을 발휘하기 어려운 단계에 도달했습니다.

**3. 글로벌 경험에 대한 갈망**
한국 시장에서의 경험은 탄탄하지만, 글로벌 환경에서 다양한 배경의 동료들과 일하며 시야를 넓히고 싶습니다.

Mark님의 제안을 받았을 때, 이것이 제가 찾던 기회라고 느꼈습니다. Zonagent는 초기 단계 스타트업으로서 빠르게 움직일 수 있고, 실제 영향력을 발휘할 수 있으며, 완전히 새로운 도메인에서 도전할 수 있는 곳입니다.

저는 내 역량을 100% 발휘할 수 있고, 내 자신 또한 폭발적으로 성장할 수 있는 곳으로 가는 것입니다.

## English Answer

I'm not leaving—I'm moving toward bigger growth.

Over the past 5 years at a Korean financial company, I've learned a great deal: building payment systems, modernizing infrastructure, and fostering team culture. However, I've recently encountered some limitations:

**1. Speed of Innovation**
Due to the nature of the financial industry, security regulations and legacy systems significantly constrain the adoption of new technologies and process improvements.

**2. Growth Ceiling**
As a tech lead, I've driven team efficiency improvements and new technology seminars, but I've reached a stage where the organizational structure limits my ability to have greater impact.

**3. Desire for Global Experience**
While my experience in the Korean market is solid, I want to broaden my perspective by working with colleagues from diverse backgrounds in a global environment.

When I received Mark's proposal, I felt this was the opportunity I'd been looking for. Zonagent is an early-stage startup where I can move fast, make real impact, and challenge myself in an entirely new domain.

I'm going where I can fully utilize 100% of my capabilities and experience explosive personal growth.

---

## 이전 예시 답변

I want to be clear—I'm not running away from something. I'm running toward something.

At Danal, I've had an incredible four years. I've grown from a backend developer to a Tech Leader, shipped systems that handle massive scale, and built a team culture I'm proud of.

But I've reached a ceiling. The scope of problems I can tackle is limited by the organization's pace and priorities. I've been doing DevOps modernization for two years, and while we've made progress, the full transformation I envisioned keeps getting delayed.

I want to work somewhere my contributions have immediate, visible impact. Somewhere I can grow into new challenges—like working with a global team, building AI-powered products, and operating in a different market. That's not possible at my current company, but it is possible here.
