## English Question

**What attracted you to our company?**

## 질문 번역

## English Answer

Three things stood out to me.

**First, the problem you're solving is real and tangible.** Making complex regulatory data clear so developers can make better decisions—that resonates with me. I've spent years making payment processes transparent and reliable. The challenge of doing the same for real estate development is genuinely exciting.

**Second, the technical challenge.** Building an AI platform that aggregates scattered data sources, processes them at scale, and delivers actionable insights—that's exactly the kind of system I've been building in fintech. The patterns are similar: high reliability requirements, data accuracy matters, and performance at scale.

**Third, the timing.** You're at an early stage where every engineer's work directly shapes the product. I've been at larger organizations where change is slow. I want to be somewhere I can build, learn, and iterate quickly—and actually see the impact of my work.
