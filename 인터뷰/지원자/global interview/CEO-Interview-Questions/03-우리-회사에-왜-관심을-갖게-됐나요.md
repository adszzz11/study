## 한글 답변

해결하려는 문제가 매력적입니다.

한국에서도 부동산 투자 관련으로 해결해야 할 문제가 많고, 이에 따른 주변 집값, 땅값의 격차를 몸소 겪어볼 수 있었습니다. 미국에서도 부동산 개발에서 인허가 리스크는 수개월의 지연과 수백만 달러의 손실을 야기할 수 있다고 들었습니다. 이 불확실성을 데이터와 AI로 명확하게 만드는 것—이것은 실제로 사람들의 의사결정을 바꾸는 의미 있는 문제입니다. 한국에서도 고민해본 문제를 글로벌한 환경에서 고민해볼 수 있는 기회를 얻는다는 것은 제게 큰 뜻이 있습니다.

Mark님이 처음 연락하셨을 때, 단순한 이직 기회가 아니라 제가 찾던 도전이라고 느꼈습니다.

---

## English Answer

The problem you're solving is genuinely attractive.

In Korea, there are many issues to be solved related to real estate investment, and I've personally experienced the disparities in housing and land prices that result from these issues. I've heard that in the US, entitlement risk in real estate development can cause delays of several months and losses of millions of dollars. Making this uncertainty clear through data and AI—this is a meaningful problem that actually changes people's decision-making. Having the opportunity to work on a problem I've thought about in Korea, but now in a global environment, holds great significance for me.

When Mark first reached out, I felt this wasn't just a job opportunity—it was the challenge I'd been looking for.

---

## 이전 예시 답변

Three things stood out to me.

**First, the problem you're solving is real and tangible.** Making complex regulatory data clear so developers can make better decisions—that resonates with me. I've spent years making payment processes transparent and reliable. The challenge of doing the same for real estate development is genuinely exciting.

**Second, the technical challenge.** Building an AI platform that aggregates scattered data sources, processes them at scale, and delivers actionable insights—that's exactly the kind of system I've been building in fintech. The patterns are similar: high reliability requirements, data accuracy matters, and performance at scale.

**Third, the timing.** You're at an early stage where every engineer's work directly shapes the product. I've been at larger organizations where change is slow. I want to be somewhere I can build, learn, and iterate quickly—and actually see the impact of my work.
