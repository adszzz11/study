# 당신의 가장 큰 약점은 무엇인가요

## English Question

**What is your biggest weakness?**

## 질문 번역

당신의 가장 큰 약점은 무엇인가요?

---

## 한글 답변

가끔 몸 관리를 못하는 것 같습니다.

업무에 집중하다 보면 시간 가는 줄 모르고 하다가, 주말이나 다른 때 내가 계획했던 일들을 하지 못한 경험이 꽤 있습니다.

현재는 책임감을 지켰다는 생각으로 동일하게 하고 있고, 이를 방지하기 위해 꾸준한 운동을 통해 기초체력을 길러 왔습니다.

---

## English Answer

Sometimes I don't manage my physical health well.

When I'm focused on work, I often lose track of time and end up not doing things I had planned for weekends or other times.

Currently, I continue the same way with the mindset that I'm fulfilling my responsibilities, and to prevent burnout, I've been building my stamina through regular exercise.

---

## 이전 예시 답변

I tend to over-engineer solutions when I first approach a problem.

Early in my career, I would design systems for scale we didn't need yet, add abstractions that weren't necessary, and spend time optimizing things that weren't bottlenecks. I once spent two weeks building a "flexible" configuration system when a simple config file would have worked fine.

I've learned to catch myself. Now, I actively ask: "What's the simplest thing that could work?" and "Are we solving today's problem or a hypothetical future one?"

Working in a startup environment actually helps with this—when you need to ship fast, there's natural pressure to keep things simple. But it's still something I consciously work on. I'd rather ship something that works and iterate than build something perfect that never launches.

스스로를 잡는 법을 배웠습니다. 이제 적극적으로 묻습니다: "작동할 수 있는 가장 간단한 것은 무엇인가?" 그리고 "오늘의 문제를 해결하는 건가 아니면 가상의 미래 문제를 해결하는 건가?"
