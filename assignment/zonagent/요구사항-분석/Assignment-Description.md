# Assignment: Agentic Document Scraper

## Background

We process municipal planning and zoning meetings. A key challenge is collecting meeting documents (minutes, agendas, agenda packets) from municipal websites. These sites vary widely in structure and format.

## Your Task

Build an **agentic** system to scrape meeting documents from Georgia municipalities.

**Target Jurisdictions:**
- Cherokee County (unincorporated)
- City of Holly Springs
- City of Alpharetta
- City of Marietta

**Document types:**
- Meeting minutes (PDF or HTML)
- Meeting agendas (PDF or HTML)
- Agenda packets (often large PDFs with multiple items)
- Video recording links (often hosted on Granicus, YouTube, or Vimeo)

**Two phases of jurisdiction onboarding:**
1. **Backfill** - Scrape historical meetings (past 1-2 years)
2. **Continuous updates** - Detect and scrape new meetings as they're posted

## Deliverables

1. **Working code** - Whatever you complete
2. **Scoping document** - What you built, what you deferred, why
3. **Architecture notes** - How does your agentic approach work?
4. **Questions** - What would you ask before production deployment?
